Bio

The Bad Reminders are a three-piece punk/rock band based in New Bedford, Massachusetts, USA. They formed in 2017 with a handful of originals mixed with classic skate-punk covers from the Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater soundtracks. In May 2019, The Bad Reminders released their DIY full-length debut, Hits and Misses. The album features a strong and dynamic rhythm section, fast-paced and frenzied guitar leads, sing-along melodies and catchy harmonies, all with lyrics that reach beyond discontent and out to the listener.

Recently, The Bad Reminders have been gaining popularity within the punk community. Their song “Leaving To Know” appears on ProRawk Records’ “...On the Rawks Compilation: Punks4Pets” a benefit compilation set to be released in February 2020 to fund no-kill shelters and combat animal cruelty. The band is currently working on new material and playing lively and energetic shows.
